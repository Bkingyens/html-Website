Brooke's Favourite Movies

My favorite movies of all time

Kiss Kiss Bang Bang

Starring Robert Downey Jr. and Val Kilmer, this movie is a hilarious and clever take on the classic detective story. The dialogue is witty and the plot is full of twists and turns. It's a must-see for fans of crime comedies.

Boondock Saints

This movie follows two Irish brothers who set out to rid Boston of its criminal underworld. The action is intense and the characters are complex. It's a gritty and violent film that will keep you on the edge of your seat.

Mean Girls

This classic teen comedy follows a high school student who infiltrates the cool clique at her new school. The writing is sharp and the performances are hilarious. It's a fun and relatable movie that never gets old.
